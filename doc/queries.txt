1. for a given route - SRC_AIRPORT - DESTINATION_AIRPORT: 
    - possible routes (airline + flight_number): direct + one-stop layover
    - puncutality of every airline on that route
    - particular flights that were delayed
    - the number of flights that were cancelled
    - the number of flights that were diverted
    - best times to fly that route (day of the week / month, etc.)

2. for a given airline - AIRLINE:
    - puncutality of the airline (cancelled, delayed, etc.)
    - hub airport(s) of that airline

3. for a given airport:
    - taxi times relative to flight take off and landing frequency.
    - punctuality of the airport, i.e. based on flights taking off and landing delays for that airport.
